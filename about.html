Imagine if...


Imagine if nonprofits had access to a broad collection of datasets and trends that could inform their decision making and contribute to the body of knowledge about our many developments and relief programs.


The Data4Good Center at the University of the Michigan School of Information (UMSI) brings together non-profit organizations(NPOs) data into larger datasets for benchmarking and trend analysis.
